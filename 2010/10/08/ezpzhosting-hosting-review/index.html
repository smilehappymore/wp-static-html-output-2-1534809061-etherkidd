ezpzhosting hosting review

Oct 8th, 2010
Leave a comment

I purchased a 2 year ezpzhosting.co.uk master reseller plan (business) and I currently would not recommend it for business use.  Read my review about their budget vps offering at this link.

I have purchased my plan in march 2010 so my plan will expire in march 2012 which I will NOT renew. As is my policy I generally do not host anything business related for 6 months of monitoring. My monitoring is done by accessing one single php page which accesses the database every 90 seconds. This tells me if the php/mysql is working as it tests the output for a specific entry with an acceptable timeout. This is more accurate in most ping/http tests available due to the way a webserver can spit out something but it might be useless, incomplete or take too long. In total I received over 24 hours of unacceptable downtime and everytime I issue a ticket it has to do with litespeed. Apparently it can serve a lot of pages but I believe ezpzhosting.co.uk management also increased their customer base to take advantage of the greater capacity which nullify it’s benefit.

My initial setup was ok as there was intro pdf which provided a great deal of information. Things that you may want to open a ticket for is 1) setup your free domain with custom nameservers like ns1-ns4; 2) purchase an ip for ssl which is NOT included. this is more professional to host whmcs if you are doing reseller business. There were other tickets but I noticed I get the best response from Dan who’s the owner I think but the rest appear to be from some outsourced support which I am use to deal with and often frustrated.

My first major issue was in July when the site would not load correctly or I should say at all. Apparently this was due to a RS1 backup that was stuck. The performance and load was unacceptable since at time I was still monitoring and so I have nothing on it to warrant such poor performance which I was migrated to another server. This solved my issue as than my email notifications became happy again. My second major issue happened October 8 at around :42am eastern when the site again would not load properly. Took 4 hours to get a response from a high level ticket who failed to do a traceroute which I requested; the response being everything is fine and if I still wanted to do a traceroute? What’s up with such response btw? How long does it take for someone to do as traceroute as requested instead of answering hours later when I was asleep whether I wanted to do something which would taken them less than 10 seconds?

I am also a bit frustrated with the load time of ezpzhosting.co.uk website which should be somewhat of an indication of problems. In additional to this their forum lists a monthly ipod review contest which they have stopped announcing any winner since May so basically the reviews you have read is solicitated and now it’s October.  It’s  my fault not for taking advantage of being a reseller but with my monitoring I wouldn’t be able to give business level hosting anyway. If you are starting out it, I would give it a try a month till you get use to cpanel/whcms and you can start reselling on a quality vps provider if you want greater reliability.

They do NOT provide any refunds so I am stuck there for a while.  They did honor a sla request of 99.9 but not sure how their monitoring is done so I can not compare it with what my monitoring is saying but  it does not appear to be accurate from their service status page since the server may be up but useless if it’s not serving php/mysql pages.  Also their sla response was that they normally calculate the sla at the end of the month but their terms state they will only honor a request 7 days of the reported incident?!  Obviously I would not rely on any sla request forthcoming…  Please do not prepay as the extra discount is not worth it for this provider.  Their website is at http://ezpzhosting.co.uk

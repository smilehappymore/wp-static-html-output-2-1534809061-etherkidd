Update: Aug 5th, 2009

This vps is hosted on vpslink.com on a link 3 Xen platform.  I use nginx as a webserver and php-fpm to handle php requests.  The link 3 has two cores and host several other sites I am developing but I am pretty sure a link 1 can handle the load on a 64mb just fine(for just ).  I might even move this at a later time if uptime is important as I am frequently using the vps for different functions which requires the web services to go down or become unstable.  I am so happy with vpslink.com and I fully endorse it (the support is lackluster but is typical of most support of other hosting companies) because the uptime is reliable.  Though if Dan LeFree ever leaves vpslink; I may also leave because he is the only one there who seems to be on top of the issues.

You can find a link in our homepage for a  lifetime 10% off your bill.  vpslink does not offer a “trial” however so choose monthly if you are unconvinced AND they do NOT offer refunds except for some circumstances.  The additional 10% is for a prepaid year only.

While vpslink.com is the cheapest (dedicated) vps provider with referral code.  For completeness; two other dedicated vps providers you may be interested in, is slicehost and linode.  Linode is ultimately the best bang for the buck while slicehost has a more professional driven base.  Again be aware of the refund policy or lack thereof.
